Keeping organized using LATCH

Today I thought it would be a great time to organize parts of my setup using the LATCH method to enchance productivity, spring cleaning in the summer we'll call it. So what is LATCH? LATCH is a method of organizing that stands for Location, Alphabet, Time, Category, and Hierarchy. These are each unique ways to organize sets of items in ways that may work better for certain things, ovall a good general rule for organizing coined by Richard Saul Wurman.
